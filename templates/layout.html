Lore

Assassin's Creed is about a conflict between Assassins and Templars spanning centuries. Players relive ancestral memories to uncover secrets and influence history. Themes include free will, power, and historical settings.

Ritual

In the Assassin's Creed lore, cutting off the ring finger is a symbolic ritual showing dedication to the Assassin Brotherhood. It signifies loyalty, sacrifice, and adherence to the Brotherhood's principles.
