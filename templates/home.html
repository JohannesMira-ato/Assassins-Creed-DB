Animus Technology


The Animus, a device that allows characters to relive the memories of their ancestors, is a central element in the series. The name "Animus" comes from Latin, meaning "soul" or "mind," reflecting its role in exploring the consciousness of the past.

Codex Pages


The Codex Pages, created by Altair Ibn-La'Ahad, are vital documents that contain essential teachings about the Assassin Brotherhood and the First Civilization. These pages reveal secrets about the powerful Apple of Eden and offer insights into the ongoing conflict between Assassins and Templars. Ultimately, the Codex symbolizes the preservation of knowledge and the rich legacy that the Assassins strive to uphold throughout history.
